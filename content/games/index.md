In my childhood, I was a huge fan of video games. I spent countless hours playing them. Nowadays, I find myself more often **creating games** than playing them. I absolutely love the process of game creation, even if finishing a game is a rare event for me.

I used to focus on creating games for the [**Playdate Console**](https://play.date/), but I've shifted my focus toward cross-platform game development. I'm now exploring [**Bevy**](https://bevy.org), a modern Rust game engine that allows me to build games for multiple platforms. The combination of Rust's safety and Bevy's flexibility has opened up new possibilities for creating games that can reach a wider audience. You can find my projects on my [**itch.io page**](https://divingavran.itch.io) and [**GitHub**](https://github.com/divin).
